## QUESTION 1. (30 Marks)

"AI-Dog", a robotic dog produced by UniqToys Limited that responds to spoken Cantonese, is a huge success in Hong Kong. In 2022, UniqToys was granted a standard patent in Hong Kong in respect of UniqX and the Messi Method. The two main claims in the patent are, in essence:

(1) An intelligent method (Messi Method) for recognizing human speech; and

Further examination revealed that DreamY comprises 2 of the 3 essential elements of UniqX, plus 1 other essential element.
